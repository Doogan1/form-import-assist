    • Land Division Survey Requirements

      Scale: Survey or map/drawing must be drawn to an appropriate scale:1" = 20', 1" = 50', 1" = 100', 1" = 200', 1" = 400', or 1" = 1000'
      The scale chosen must best represent the property and its improvements.


      Parent Parcel Information:

      Show boundaries of the parent parcel as of 3/31/97.

      Include legal descriptions for the parent parcel, the remaining parent tract, and any new parcels.

      Proposed Divisions:

      Clearly label proposed divisions and include dimensions and acreage of each new parcel.

      Improvements:

      Show locations of existing buildings, driveways, wells, septic systems, etc.
      Include setbacks from buildings to property lines.

      Easements and Rights-of-Way:

      Show existing and proposed easements (e.g., utility easements) and right-of-ways.

      Surveyor's Seal:

      Survey must be sealed by a Michigan licensed surveyor.
